Dem Wrasslin Boyz

Your Tag Team Hosts

Two South side Marietta guys who've been talking wrasslin since 6th grade. Now they're doing it on mic.

TP
Co-Host & Creator

The P.O.T.

Originally from Toledo, Ohio, The P.O.T. moved to Marietta around age 4 and became a wrestling fan the moment he saw "Nature Boy" Ric Flair on screen. Having to sneak around just to watch it only made him love wrasslin more — and that passion sparked Welcome 2 Wrasslin.

Mount Rushmore
"Nature Boy" Ric Flair · Hulk Hogan
Stone Cold Steve Austin · The Rock
BE
Co-Host & Technical Lead

Big E

Born and raised on the South side of Marietta, Big E was about 5 when he fell in love with wrestling — styling his hair like Ricky Morton and watching NWA every Friday night from 8pm to 1am. He handles all the editing and media production, and is undefeated wrestling his pillows.

Mount Rushmore
Hulk Hogan · "Nature Boy" Ric Flair
Stone Cold Steve Austin · Shawn Michaels (HBK)
Their Origin Story

6th grade. Gym class.
A Sting T-shirt.

Dem Wrasslin Boyz met at Marietta Junior High. The P.O.T. spotted Big E rocking a Sting shirt — Sting had just won the world title in 1990 — and knew immediately he was talking to a fellow fan. A bond over wrestling that neither could form with anyone else. Thirty-something years later, they're still at it. On mic.
